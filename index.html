Research & Publications

I am interested in researching factors related to climate change and how those factors affect human activity and health. These include extreme weather events, agricultural practices, and urbanization. I have experience with GIS, remote sensing, and programming in various languages for SWE. My research topics include land use/land cover change, climate change, and data visualization. As an undergraduate studying Geographic Information Systems and Computer Science, I aim to research important trends in the environment using new technologies. Additionally, I enjoy translating my research findings into useful data visualizations by creating web applications.
