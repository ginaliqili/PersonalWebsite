Research & Publications

My research area examines extreme weather events due to climate change and how those events affect human health and activity. I am particularly interested in extreme heat events and public health. In my master's thesis, I define heat waves for communities in the US and then create a web map application to forecast and visualize heat-wave mortalities in real-time for each US community.

My work also involves remote sensing, GIS, software development, and machine learning.
